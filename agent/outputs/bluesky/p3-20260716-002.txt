Gartner's 2022 call: voice AI cuts contact center labor by $80B by 2026.

It's 2026. $0.40/call (AI) vs $7-12/call (human). Unit economics check out.

The gap: most ops run voice AI at 15% of volume, calling it "production."

You can't save $80B if the human queue is still 85%.

The $80B landed — unevenly.
