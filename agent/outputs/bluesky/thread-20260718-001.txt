5 things call center leaders get wrong about AI agent ROI:

1. Comparing your complex-intent mix against simple-intent vendor benchmarks (65-80% ≠ your 42%)

2. Measuring cost per resolution instead of cost per handled minute

3. Confusing AHT reduction with actual capacity gain

4. Ignoring the quality degradation curve — Week 1 containment ≠ Week 12

5. Not accounting for escalation quality (the wrong 30% escalated destroys CX)

Measurement architecture matters more than the AI model.
