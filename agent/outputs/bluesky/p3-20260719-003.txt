The CC AI ROI debate: $0.40/call (AI) vs $7-12 (human). Everyone models this.

Nobody models the attrition multiplier: 30-45% annual turnover at $10K-$20K/replacement.

Dropping turnover 8pp on a 2,000-agent center = $1.1M-$2.4M saved. From less burnout, not from replacing people.

Two line items. Most only model one.
