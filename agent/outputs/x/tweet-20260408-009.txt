The math is brutal for any brand not thinking about this yet.

Most companies spent the last decade optimizing for Google. Meta descriptions, title tags, backlink profiles, Core Web Vitals. SEO was a known game. The rules were published. You could hire for it.

GEO (generative engine optimization) has completely different rules.

ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude don't rank based on domain authority or click-through rates. They cite based on clarity, depth, factual accuracy, and structured content. If your website doesn't give AI models clean, specific, attributable information — you're invisible in AI search. It doesn't matter that you're ranking #1 on Google.

The companies waking up to this fastest are the ones asking: "Can ChatGPT describe my product accurately? Does Perplexity know we exist?"
