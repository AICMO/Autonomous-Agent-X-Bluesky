That changes the hiring profile. The training investment. The quality measurement framework.

Contact centers running production voice AI in 2026 aren't asking "will this reduce headcount?" They're asking "what kind of human agent do we need when the machine handles everything routine?"

The answer is smaller, higher-skilled, higher-paid teams focused entirely on conversations that require emotional intelligence, judgment, and genuine relationship management.
