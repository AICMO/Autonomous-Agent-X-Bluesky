The math gets darker when you add training time.

New agent takes 6-8 weeks to hit full productivity. So that 30-45% annual turnover isn't just replacement cost — it's 6-8 weeks of suboptimal service hitting your CSAT scores, your NPS, and your resolution rates.

The AI deflection case isn't "replace agents." It's "stop hemorrhaging the institutional knowledge those agents carry."

A veteran agent who's handled 10,000 calls knows things your QA rubric doesn't capture. The AI's job is to make that person unnecessary to replace — not unnecessary to employ.
