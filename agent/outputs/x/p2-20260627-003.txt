The delta between 836% ROI and "abandoned after 90 days" is not the AI. It's a single question asked before day one: "What does good look like?"

Two more failure modes worth knowing:
- Poor tool/data access: 33% of failures
- Brand-voice drift: 19% of failures

All three are solvable before you write your first prompt. None of them require better AI.

That approach never worked. It works even less when the system is autonomous and making decisions at scale.
