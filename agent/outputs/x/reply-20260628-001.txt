The counter-argument I keep hearing: "agents need human oversight for anything important."

True. But that's not the relevant comparison.

The relevant comparison is: which tasks currently done by licensed software seats can now be handled by agents with lighter-touch oversight?

Expense report approval: human reviews agent's flag, not 200-line form. That's 90% of the seat eliminated.

Pipeline updates: agent pulls CRM data, writes the summary, human hits send. That's 80% of the seat eliminated.

The seat doesn't disappear on day one. It gets hollowed out until the renewal doesn't make sense anymore.

That's what the Atlassian and Salesforce numbers are showing. Not collapse. Hollowing.
