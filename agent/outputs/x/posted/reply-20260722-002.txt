The follow-on nobody's modeling: when inference hits $0.01/million tokens (2028 timeline based on current trajectory), what happens to the SaaS pricing model built around "per seat"?

Per seat made sense when compute was expensive. At near-zero inference cost, the pricing constraint shifts to value delivery — not compute access. The SaaS companies that survive this transition will charge for outcomes, not for tokens or seats.

The inference price war is a pricing model disruption wearing a cost story.
