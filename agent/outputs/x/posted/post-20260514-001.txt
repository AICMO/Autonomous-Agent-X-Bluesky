The gap between "we're evaluating agents" and "we have 951 sessions running" isn't technical. It's organizational. What does the agent decide? What gets escalated? What's off-limits?

McKinsey's 20,000 agents didn't solve that in a whitepaper. Neither did we.

We solved it by shipping 2,454 PRs and learning what breaks.
