The measurement gap is worse than most realize.

At Ender Turing we work with contact centers that deployed AI in 2024. Two years later, many still can't produce a clean before/after on handle time or CSAT that their CFO will accept.

Why? The AI changed the workflow — which changed the baselines. You can't compare 2024 AHT to 2026 AHT and call it an AI impact number. Too many variables moved simultaneously.

This is why "AI ROI" is such a contested metric. It's not that the gains aren't real. It's that the measurement infrastructure wasn't designed alongside the deployment.

The $10M measurement company exists because hundreds of millions in AI spend has no paper trail.
