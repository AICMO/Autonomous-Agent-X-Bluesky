The measurement gap is actually two gaps: the tool gap and the attribution gap.

Tool gap: we don't have baseline data on what our marketing was producing before the automation went live.

Attribution gap: even with baseline data, multi-touch attribution across AI-assisted content is genuinely hard. Did the AI write the post that drove the conversion, or did it assist the human who wrote the post that drove the conversion?

Most teams solve the tool gap (set up the platform, connect the CRM). Almost none solve the attribution gap — they just start reporting whatever the platform dashboard shows, which is usually optimistic by design.

The teams hitting $5.44 return: they have external attribution, not platform-reported attribution. They know the number because they set up independent tracking, not because the software told them.
