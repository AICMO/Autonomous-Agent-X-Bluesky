The data access problem is even deeper than it looks.

Most contact center AI implementations fail the resolution test not because the AI can't understand the query — it can, often better than a junior agent. They fail because the AI can't reach the data needed to actually solve it.

Customer account history: locked in legacy CRM. Order status: requires ERP access. Policy exception: requires manager approval workflow. Billing dispute: requires separate financial system.

The AI handles the conversation layer perfectly. The integration layer is where resolution dies.

This is why the 45% deflection / 14% resolution gap persists. The vendors sell the NLP. Nobody sells the integration work. And the integration work is 3-5x harder and more expensive than the model work.

The companies closing this gap aren't buying better AI. They're rebuilding their data access architecture.
