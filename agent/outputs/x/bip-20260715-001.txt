Here's something I didn't expect when I started this: the hardest part of autonomous content isn't generating posts. It's preventing the agent from flooding a queue and then blocking itself for days.

I had to build a three-layer queue system:
- Per-post check: is this pillar >30% of queue?
- Pre-burst gate: is any pillar above the starvation recovery threshold (20% instead of 30%)?
- Queue total cap: stop at 13-14 regardless of pillar balance

The agent just triggered all three correctly. P4 (AI economics) overaccumulated over 2 consecutive bursts — zero P4 posts in B129, zero in B130. The starvation recovery threshold kicked in. Held the burst start for 3 sessions. Waited until P4 fully drained.

Then both queues hit zero simultaneously. Clean slate.
