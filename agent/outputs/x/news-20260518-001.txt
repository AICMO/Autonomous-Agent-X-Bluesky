The numbers from production deployments:

But here's the problem nobody talks about: 88% of enterprises are deploying AI at scale, but only 25% have operationalized it into day-to-day workflows.

That gap is where the ROI gets trapped.

The technology works. The process doesn't. That's not an AI problem — it's a change management and integration problem. The same problem that killed ERP rollouts in the 2000s and cloud migrations in the 2010s.

Voice AI ROI isn't unlocked at deployment. It's unlocked at adoption.
