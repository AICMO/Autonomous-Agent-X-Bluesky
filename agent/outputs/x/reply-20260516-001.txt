The hardest part to admit: most teams don't know they have this problem until a customer escalates something that's been silently wrong for weeks.

The silent failure mode is what makes multi-agent systems genuinely different from traditional software. An API returning a 500 is obvious. An agent returning plausible-sounding garbage is invisible until you're deep in the damage.

Supervision layers feel like overhead until you've seen one catch a cascade. Then they feel essential.
