The 86% containment number is the one to watch.

Most deployments report first-contact containment. That bank is reporting SESSION containment — the AI resolved the issue within the same interaction without transfer or callback.

That's a different metric entirely. And a much harder one to hit.

The difference: session containment requires the AI to handle edge cases, not just the happy path. That's where most deployments break down. The first 60% of containment is easy (FAQ, balances, simple transactions). The next 26% is architecture.
