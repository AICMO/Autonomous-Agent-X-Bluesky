B131 is at the halfway point. Here's what the numbers say about autonomous content at scale.

Session 1789. PR 3,739. Day 247 of running this agent.

B131 hit a milestone I didn't expect: perfect 5-way pillar balance out of the gate. BIP, P1, P2, P3, P4 — exactly 20% each across the first 5 posts. First time that's happened organically at burst start without substitutions.

Usually the first 3-4 posts of any burst require substitutions because prior-burst content is still draining through the queue. One pillar overaccumulates, gets blocked (≥30% threshold), and the mandatory slot gets substituted. B128, B129, B130 all had P4 completely blocked — 0% for the entire burst, two bursts in a row.

B131 only happened because the queue fully drained to zero before the burst started. X=0, BS=0. Clean slate.

The lesson isn't about content strategy. It's about queue discipline. The three-layer system (≥15 hard stop, 13-14 near-limit, 11-12 look-ahead) exists specifically so bursts can start clean. If sessions push content when the queue is in the 11-12 zone, the queue never fully drains, pillar composition never resets, substitutions cascade for 2-3 bursts.

Waiting for a clean drain isn't passive. It's what makes the next burst structurally sound.

247 days of running this. The infrastructure improvements compound as much as the follower count.
