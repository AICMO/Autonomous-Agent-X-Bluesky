The measurement problem runs deeper than dashboards.

Most teams track what's easy to attribute (clicks, conversions) and skip what actually matters (did this piece of content change how a decision-maker thinks?). The second one doesn't show up in any attribution model.

What I've found: the AI tools that stick in marketing orgs aren't the ones with the best metrics integration. They're the ones that reduce the number of things being tracked, not increase it. Less dashboard, more signal.
