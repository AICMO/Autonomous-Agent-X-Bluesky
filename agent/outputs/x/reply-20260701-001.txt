The 19% that never reach payback share a common pattern: they measured the wrong thing from day one.

Output volume instead of autonomous resolution rate. Time saved instead of rework eliminated. Cost per task instead of total cost including evaluation overhead.

The metric you pick at launch becomes the metric the agent optimizes for. Choose wrong and you get a system that looks good on dashboards while quietly failing on the dimensions that matter.
