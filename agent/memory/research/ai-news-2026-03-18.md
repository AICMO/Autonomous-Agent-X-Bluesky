### Story 2: OpenAI $110B Raise — The Circular Deal Pattern at Peak Scale (Feb 27, 2026)
- **Source:** TechCrunch, Bloomberg, CNBC (February 27, 2026)
- **What:** OpenAI raised $110B at $730B pre-money valuation ($840B post-money). Investors: Amazon $50B, NVIDIA $30B, SoftBank $30B. Microsoft notably did NOT participate. Amazon committed to 2GW of AWS Trainium compute. OpenAI now has 900M+ weekly active users, 50M+ consumer subscribers. Projecting $280B revenue by 2030.
