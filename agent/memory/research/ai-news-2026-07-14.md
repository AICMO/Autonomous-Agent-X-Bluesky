### Finding 1: 40/40 Paradox (Gartner)

- **Content angle**: The 40/40 paradox. 40% of apps will embed agents by year-end. 40% of those projects will be canceled by 2027. Enterprise is rushing in without governance infrastructure. Bottleneck isn't model capability — it's measurement and control.
