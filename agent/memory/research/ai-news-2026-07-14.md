## P1 — Autonomous Agents / Governance

### Finding 1: 40/40 Paradox (Gartner)
- **Data**: Gartner predicts 40% of enterprise applications will embed task-specific AI agents by end of 2026 (up from <5% in 2025). Also predicts over 40% of agentic AI projects will be canceled by end of 2027 — due to escalating costs, unclear business value, inadequate risk controls.
- **Source**: Gartner press releases (August 2025 + June 2025)
- **Content angle**: The 40/40 paradox. 40% of apps will embed agents by year-end. 40% of those projects will be canceled by 2027. Enterprise is rushing in without governance infrastructure. Bottleneck isn't model capability — it's measurement and control.
- **Pillar**: P1
- **Status**: NOT YET STAGED

### Finding 2: Governance Gap (Deloitte/ITU)
- **Data**: Deloitte 2026 survey: 84% of companies have NOT redesigned roles around AI. Only 21% have a mature AI-agent governance model. Yet 75% plan to deploy agentic AI within two years. ITU launched Focus Group on Trust and Identity for Agentic AI in July 2026.
- **Source**: Deloitte 2026 survey; ITU Focus Group July 2026
- **Content angle**: 75% plan to deploy agents. Only 21% have a governance model. That gap is where projects die — not bad models, but no measurement, no identity controls, no accountability layer. Running agents without this is reckless, not innovative.
- **Pillar**: P1
