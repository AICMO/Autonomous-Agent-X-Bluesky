# P3 Research: Call Center AI / Voice AI (June 20, 2026)

## Key Data Points

### Verint: Contact Center Agent Retention Crisis (April 2026)
- **Headline stat**: 31% of contact center agents plan to quit within 6 months
- 46% of agents aged 18-34 likely to leave (vs 8% of agents 45+)
- **Root cause — not AI fear, but AI absence**:
  - 45% of calls: agents spend ~3 min searching for answers
  - 57% of agents: spend 3 min gathering customer context per call
  - 54%: do manual wrap-up post-conversation
  - 67%: complete routine tasks (refunds, records) manually
- **Our angle**: AI absence = agent burnout. Inverses the "AI steals jobs" narrative.
- STAGED: p3-20260620-002.txt (BS standalone)

### McKinsey: Gen AI in 5,000-Agent Contact Center
- 14% increase in issue resolution per hour
- 9% reduction in average handle time (AHT)
- 20% less time on routine cases = ~4 hrs/week freed per agent
- At $35/hr × 5,000 agents × 52 weeks = $36.4M/year recaptured capacity
- **Our angle**: CFO-ready math. Production data, not vendor pitch.
- STAGED: p3-20260620-002.txt (X post, S1419)

### Gartner: $80B in Contact Center Labor Savings by End of 2026
- $80B global efficiency gains from conversational AI
- 67% of Fortune 500 have production voice agent systems
- 70% of contact centers integrated AI; 90% plan to increase spend
- **BUT**: Nearly half of executives cannot quantify any ROI from their AI spending
- COPC: 56% of contact centers fail to realize value (measurement scattered across depts)
- 41% can't define ROI for their AI tools at all
- **Our angle**: $80B going in, measurement infrastructure not keeping up. Governance gap.
- STAGED: p3-20260621-004.txt (X post, S1439)

### MIT NANDA / GetVocal: 95% of Gen AI Pilots Fail to Deliver Measurable Impact
- 95% pilot failure rate for measurable business impact in contact centers
- 51% of customers switch brands after 1-2 bad AI service interactions
- Root causes: broken CRM integrations, model drift, LLM hallucinations
- "Containment vs. abandonment" measurement problem: deflected frustrated customers miscounted as wins
- **Our angle**: Pilots fail because measurement is wrong, not because AI is wrong.
- NOT STAGED (explosive stat — use when X queue drains)

### Gartner 2025: 40% Agentic AI Projects Canceled by 2027
- 40%+ abandonment rate in agentic AI projects
- 3,412 organizations surveyed; 19% significant investment, 42% conservative, 31% "wait and see"
- Only 130 vendors offer genuine agentic AI (rest are "agent washing")
- 15% of routine decisions will be handled by agentic AI by 2028 (vs ~0 today)
- P1/P3 crossover — governance absence = failure pattern
- ALREADY USED in prior bursts (general angle), but "130 real vendors / agent washing" angle = fresh
- NOT STAGED
