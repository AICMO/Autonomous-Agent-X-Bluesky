## P3: Call Center AI / Voice AI / CX

### Hook 1: $7-12 → $0.40 per-call cost cliff (STRONGEST P3 HOOK)
- **Stat**: AI voice agents cost $0.07-0.15/minute versus $29-42/hour for a US-based human agent. Per-call cost drops from $7-12 (human) to $0.40 (voice AI) — 17-30x reduction.
- **Source**: Nextiva / Ringly.io (2026 statistics roundup)
- **Angle**: Not just "AI is cheaper" — the unit economics are now so asymmetric that delaying deployment is a quantified monthly loss. Every month of delay at $7/call vs $0.40/call is easy math. The question is no longer whether to deploy but how fast.
- **Status**: STAGED (p3-20260611-001.txt, S1296)

### Hook 2: 91% under executive pressure (Gartner)
- **Stat**: 91% of customer service and support leaders are under executive pressure to implement AI (Gartner 2026 survey).
- **Source**: Ringly.io citing Gartner 2026
- **Angle**: The mandate is arriving from the top, not the bottom — inverts the typical adoption path. Leaders without implementation knowledge are scrambling. The gap between "the board says implement AI" and "ops team says which one?" is where projects die.
- **Status**: READY

### Hook 3: 85-90% CSAT in 90 days
- **Stat**: Well-configured voice AI deployments hit 85-90% CSAT on fully resolved calls, with 50%+ containment rates. CSAT scores increase 8-15 points within 90 days of deployment.
- **Source**: IrisAgent / CallBotics 2026 benchmark data
- **Angle**: The 90-day window is the hidden lever — most enterprises stall waiting for 12-month proof. The ROI is in the first quarter. Everything after is optimization.
- **Status**: READY
