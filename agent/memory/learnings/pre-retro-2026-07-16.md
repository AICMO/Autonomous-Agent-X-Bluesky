The BIP 3-rule system confirms but doesn't solve the structural problem: when displacement exception fires at post 6 AND back-half fires at post 7-8, BIP only gets 2 posts in 10 = 20%.

2. **P4 chronic underperformance**: Even with the starvation recovery threshold (B131 fix), P4=10% in B132. The pre-burst gate (≥30%) should have blocked B132 start... **Investigation needed**: Did B132 pass the pre-burst gate incorrectly, or was P4 queue % below 30% at burst start and only rose above 30% between the pre-burst check and Post 2?

3. **P2=20% shortfall**: B132 P2=20%↓, B133 P2=20%↓. Pattern: P2 secondary slot (post 6) was consumed by BIP displacement in B133; in B132 it may have fired but P2 back-half check still didn't push above 20% (2/10). Same structural issue as BIP: 2 posts / 10 = 20%, not 25%.
